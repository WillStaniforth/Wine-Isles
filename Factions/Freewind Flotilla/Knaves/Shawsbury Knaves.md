The Shawsbury Knaves were a band of halfling bandits that caused havoc for the Gilded Scales company as they expanded their trade routes south of Maltha.

From the hidden and relatively fortified position of Shawsbury, the Knaves peformed daring raids on important Malthan trade between Maltha and [[Cantalicia]]. 

The Knaves were crushed beneath the boot of Gilded Scale Mercenaries as they swept south across the coast and forced halflings out of home as they searched for the lost treasures that had been stolen over the years of raids.

[[Tezu Autumnfound]]'s master was once a member of the Knaves. Tezu has often wondered about the location of the Knave's Bounty. Recently, Tezu set out on a dangerous expedition to the ruins of Shawsbury to try to find a clue. Just as he was on the cusp of a discovery, he was captured by the Gilded Scales and hauled to a prison in Cantalicia.
