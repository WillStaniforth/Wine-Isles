Since the party have slain Bavrazi in [[Devil's Cay]], she must fulfill her pact to protect the treasures of Evelina Silver-Teeth a different way. She has rematerialized within Caina and plots her attempts at reclaiming the treasure.

Her plan is to hire doppelgangers to infiltrate the crew of the Kara II to set up a summoning circle for Bavrazi and silently slay the crew in their sleep.
