Besmara searches for a new champion, a new hurricane king. As a test, she wants one person to seek out and collect all 8 pieces of the original Pirate king's regalia. The pirate king's regalia are the original items owned and used by Gunnarik Thunderhoof during his original reign of terror.

There are eight pieces of the hurricane king's regalia:

* Compass
* Cutlass
* Pistol
* Dagger
* Jacket
* Hat
* Bandolier
* Star of Absalom
