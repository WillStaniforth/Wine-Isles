Letter 1:

Esteemed Commodore Valverde,

I appreciate you're platitudes regarding my health, but I am under no illusion as to my ailment's progress. Regardless, I understand that the situation in Alegria is dire and we risk losing our foothold in Kazano.

As you are by no doubt already aware, the situation in Triseze has deteriorated somewhat, and we have sent a small fleet headed by Commodore Valente aboard the Iron Tide. The conflict in Syrano has been diverting a large proportion of Andaceres' fleet north, and what we have left is patrolling the seas of Secinia to protect the city. I am expecting Commodore Valente to return by the 8th of Gozran, and shall send his fleet to reinforce Alegria Keep swiftly upon their return. Barring any unforeseen circumstances, they should be with you no later than the 17th of Gozran.

In the meantime, I have recently received an "Arcanaut", the newest war machine dreamed up by Archon Ramos. One alone has the firepower of an arcanoflame cannon to assault vessels, whilst being nimble enough to engage the enemy on foot. I cannot launch it today as the arcanists need to make some adjustments before it is sent into the field, but will send it on a swift vessel as soon as I am able.

I trust that under your capable leadership you can hold for this short time before reinforcements arrive.

Yours sincerely,
Contessa Rivera di Andaceres



Letter 2:

Commodore,

The spate of triton incursions onto the coastal settlements of Kazano are increasing further. Across the different attacks, the same patterns are repeating. Primarily, the tritons will arrive in the dead of night and take hostages from the night workers, guards, and drunkards still about at that hour. They will demand treasure for the release of the hostages. In towns where treasure has been refused, they haven't pushed further but simply taken their hostages back below the waves with them. Where the demands have been met they have taken the treasure and released most hostages, but still kept a handful.

We had assumed that they had drowned as soon as they were taken under but on the 39th Shellight, the mayor of Bergara received a sending from a cleric of Abadar visiting from Unarith. In the sending, he claimed that they were being kept alive for some sort of feast or ritual. The mayor has not received any sendings since.

Furthermore, we have just received shipment of the diving bell from Andaceres, and it is ready to be moved to the Sternhammer upon your return to Alegria Keep.

Yours sincerely,
Captain Santos
