In searching for Vitreus Huma, he got a tip off indicating that he had fled to the Wine Isles. With such a broad place to search, Ampere thought to begin his search at Port Selos. The port has made a name for itself as a flourishing hub of trade and luxury, with many wealthy nobles holidaying there. The destination is known for it's dyes, it's hunting, and the wilderness of Isla Cereme teeming with Lord Vossk's collection of rare monstrosities.

After travelling to Port Selos and finding a place to stay, he realised how expensive the destination truly is. To get by while he searches, he has begun taking up small jobs he can find in the city. Recently, down on his luck, he has accepted a job with two other adventurers as they attempt to raid the legendary Lord Vossk's manor.

What Ampere knows about the island:
* Port Selos is a honeypot for rich folk looking for a luxurious place to spend their wealth.
* The rest of the island is a nature reserve owned by Lord Vossk.
* Nobles will often pay for dinosaur hunting licences and head into the wilderness for sport.
* A lot of the island is tropical rainforest, and the climate is very humid and rainy.
* Vitreous Huma is used to the high life and would look for somewhere nice to hide from those that seek revenge.
* The Port is ruled by seven merchant princes. They live in palaces in the merchant's ward, and often host visitors looking for favours or trade.
* There are two ways out of the city; by boat, or by the Wildgate into the reserve.
* The city really doesn't want people going into the reserve without being monitored.
* The trappers obviously have some  way of communicating with each other across the island; the more obviously you break into the reserve and cause a commotion, the higher alert the reserve will be.
* You think it will be pretty impossible to break into the manor if the alert level is too high.
