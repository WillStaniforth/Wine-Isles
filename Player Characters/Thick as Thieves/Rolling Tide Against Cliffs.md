What Tide knows about Isla Cereme:
* The island has a single city on it's northernmost point, Port Selos.
* Port Selos is a wealthy trading port known for it's luxuries; dyes, gemstones, and dinosaur meat.
* Things are quite expensive in Port Selos, other than the Old City ward.
* The rest of the island is a nature reserve owned by Lord Vossk.
* There are two ways out of the city; by boat, or by the Wildgate into the reserve.
* The city really doesn't want people going into the reserve without being monitored.
* All sorts of nasty monstrosities roam the island.
* Somewhere within the reserve is Lord Vossk's manor.
* There are some roads throughout the reserve. While these provide the quickest ways across the island, there is almost no way to travel them stealthily.
* About two "cunning plans to get into the reserve undetected" ago, Vhulas went undercover as a trapper in the reserve. You have not seen him since.
