A quaint and peaceful farming village nestled near lush rolling fields. Tenford's economy thrives on agriculture, with its residents cultivating crops like wheat, barley, and vegetables. The village square serves as a bustling marketplace every oathday, where farmers gather to barter and sell their produce. Merchants often frequent Tenford to arrange bulk trades for other coastal cities, leveraging Port Specter as their trade hub. The community here is tight-knit, and Tenford is known for its annual Firstbloom Harvest Festival, where locals showcase their finest crops and host contests like "The Largest Turnip" and "Fastest Pie-Eater."
