The town of Alegria is a small one, mostly supporting the large keep at the north-eastern tip of the island. 

Important locations:

***Alegria Port.*** The port is the largest and most important employer in the town. Heavily guarded by Gilded Scales guards, the docks supply the town and the keep with supplies. There are usually a handful of Gilded Scale military vessels in port.

*Arriving at the town of Alegria, your ship sails smoothly into a large open harbour. There is a rushed business to the port, with sailors and dockworkers hurriedly making their way about on the dock and the jetties. On the western side of the harbour, two large wooden buildings hold crates and cargo being offloaded from vessels. The whole harbour is dominated by the imposing fortress on the eastern side, its high stone walls casting long shadows across the harbour in the early morning sun. As the ship begins to dock, a cloud cover begins to roll in, and you hear light sound of rain tapping against the wooden deck as it begins to fall.*


***The Tipsy Trader Inn.*** The only inn on the island, it does not have to keep up glamourous appearances to get custom. It's only patrons are sailors in port for a short time. Rather than individual rooms, it is a bunkhouse with a large room full of bunkbeds.

*The only inn on the isle, the Tipsy Trader looks dull, dark, and unfriendly. Large stones make up most of the building's outer structure, and it's difficult to see through the curtained windows.*

*Entering the tavern through the dirty wooden door, you're welcomed by a dull murmurs of quiet conversation and the smell of alcohol. The bartender is trying to catch a spider and makes no effort to acknowledge your presence.*



***Abadar's Scales.*** Temple of Abadar. One of the larger buildings in the town. 


***Tower of Tides.*** Lighthouse and temple of Gozreh.
