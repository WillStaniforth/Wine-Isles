Factions



Locations



**Green Dragon Lair**
The hidden lair of a green dragon that makes the lakes of Northern Secinia it's home. They hide all of their treasure here that they claim from the lighthouse.

**Insectoid Caverns**
Once a smuggling stash in a small cove used by pirates, it has since become completely infested with giant insects. It provides a route through to the island.

**Necromancer's Tomb**
The tomb of an ancient and powerful necromancer. The jungle around this region is crawling with more undead than usual.

**Nightfont Ruins**
The ruins of an ancient shadow worshiping cult.

**Pepperspring**
A spring settlement inhabited by sprites that became trapped outside of the feywild generations ago.

**R'Eeb'R'Eeb**
A tribe of grung that worship the nearby green dragon.

**Rith**
A Kua-Toa settlement within caves beneath the mud flats. They worship beasts of the lake.

**Sirensong Beacon**
This magically enchanted lighthouse lures sailors towards the rocks near the cliff

**Slickback Tribe**
A lizardfolk tribe that live and hunt in Nightfont lake.
