Factions

Vezael and Kwolosh. Two powerful fey that were tricked to the cursed isle of Lost Secinia by an archfey that hated their fighting. Their sylvan immortality now twisted and cursed, they are doomed to fight eachother for eternity on the isle.


Locations
**Cindershard**
An ancient obelisk that houses powerful primordial fires.

**Crustcrown**
The volcano fortress of a clan of fire giants.

**Circle of Nambu**
A friendly circle of myconids protected from the ravaging gnolls by raging rivers.

**Coeurl Lair**
The lair of a cunning displacer beast coeurl. It is slippery enough that it has not yet been killed by the gnolls, and it hunts the gnolls for sport.

**Gnoll Warbands**
While never staying in one location, they patroll the north-west of lost Secinia constantly hunting down the broken archfey Kwolosh.

**Green Hag Lair**
The lair of a cunning green hag, who hides from the gnolls through illusions and beneath thick plants.

**Harmony's Last Breath**
An enclave of gnolls led by shamans that resist the curse of hunger on their kind.

**Kwoloshgrave**
The original site of Kwolosh's demise. The cursed fey has since revived hundreds of times throughout the region.

**Lost Mine**
An ancient lost mine.

**Red Dragon Lair**
The volcano home of Ignus Magna, the sibling/parent of Ignus Rex.

**Research Lodge**
A research lodge established by a university in a far off land. They have established a teleportation circle here.

**Ruins of Skyspire**
An ancient Secinian city more well preserved than it has any right to be, atop one of the natural pillars.

**Skyspire Mine**
A network of mines running through the pillars, with ancient bridges connecting them.

**Temple of Elion**
A temple to a long lost deity of exploration.

**Vezaelgrave**
The original site of Vezael's demise.
