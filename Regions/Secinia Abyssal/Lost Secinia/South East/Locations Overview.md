Factions:
**Blackscale tribe**
A tribe of lizardfolk that slink stealthily through the jungle.

**P'Oosh'L'Oosh**
A tribe of slippery Grung that don't appreciate settlers in their territory.

**Boulderfall Clan**
A clan of proud hill giants that live in the mountains.

**Gilded Scales**
The Gilded Scales have an exploratory interest in the island, and they are stationed at fort Tristeze.


Locations:

**Alicos**
A small triton dominance. This dominance is particularly hostile to outsiders. Any slain by the dominance are given to the sea serpent they worship.

**Ancient Statue**
An ancient crumbling statue in a state of disrepair.

**Basilisk Lair**
The lair of an ancient gemstone basilisk, hidden behind a waterfall in a cliff face. It patrols the nearby jungle and takes its unfortunate prey home to feast.

**Boneyard**
The resting bones of an ancient colossal creature.

**Blackscale Cave**
A half-flooded cavern lair of the blackscale lizardfolk.

**Boulderfall**
The mountainous home of the hill giants upon one of the highest peaks in the region.

**Drowned Ruins**
The ruins of an old settlement completely sank beneath the marsh.

**Fort Tristeze (Trist-ay-zee)**
A fort town of the Gilded Scales. The townsfolk are miserable in their new home due to conditions and grung attacks.

**Huitzilin**
A sect of the Coils of Ydersius driven mad by a curse afflicting them since they arrived on the island. They make their home in an ancient stone ziggurat.

**Joni's Watch**
An ancient abandoned lighthouse.

**Megari Ruins**
A settlement from a hundred years back that was overrun by the nasties living on the island.

**Mirrorlake Shrine**
An ancient stone shrine overlooking the glassy mirrorlake. The mirrorlake itself gets it's unusual reflective properties from runoff silver from the Silverheart mine.

**P'Oosh'L'Oosh**
The home of the grung tribe of the same name, in an ancient stone temple built on a small lake.

**Ritual Point**
A temple built with a large flat overhang that drops hundreds of feet to the rocky ground below.

**Sea Emperor's Temple**
A temple to an ancient and forgotten deity of the sea.

**Serpent's Lair**
The lair of an ancient sea serpent worshiped by Alicos.

**Silverheart Mine**
An old silver mine that has been abandoned due to flooding. There are still veins of silver for adventurers willing to risk it.

**Temple of Amaznen**
An ancient temple to a forgotten deity of knowledge. Almost all of the texts held here are ruined.

**Tower of Dorn**
An ancient tower used as a forward watch point of the Gilded Scales.
