Factions



Locations

**Avela**
A dwarven citadel several hundreds of years abandoned and taken over by the circle of Deelbab.

**B'Lon**
A grung faction that lurk deep within the heart of the jungle. They war with the Razorteeth tribe over the font of desire.

**Circle of Deelbab**
A circle of myconids that has been corrupted by an abyssal touch. They are significantly more aggressive than others of their kind.

**Clockwork Drakes**
Nests of self-repairing clockwork dragons that protect the sanctum.

**Coven of the Beating Heart**
A trio of hags that have long plotted to break into the sanctum, but have yet to achieve this goal.

**Cult of Rot**
A cult of survivors on the island that have spent too much time within the mushroom forests.

**Dhol Turuhm**
An abandoned Wine Haven destroyed by volcanic activity.

**Flintclaw Tribe**
A tribe of kobolds that fashion weapons and armour from flint and live in underground caverns with large lakes.

**Font of Desire**
A mystic font that is said to grant wishes.

**Hydra's Lair**
The lair of a great hydra that is worshiped by the razorteeth tribe.

**Razorteeth Tribe**
A lizardfolk tribe that live in the marshes along the coast. They war with the B'Lon grung over the font of desire.

**Sanctum**
The secret in the heart of Secinia. Protected by a purging field that dispels magic that passes through it and instantly kills anything living.

**Secinian Ruin**
An ancient Secinian palace and one home of the architect of the Sanctum.

**Stonetooth Tribe**
A tribe of kobolds in the mountains. They worship a clockwork drake that lives nearby, and aim to create clockwork contraptions to impress it.

**Temple of Moloch**
An ancient temple to moloch. The original worshipers are long dead, but it is a point of pilgrimage to those wanting to prove themselves to the archdevil.

**Terragoza**
A failed attempt by Unarith to establish a settlement in Lost Secinia. 

**Veiled Pass**
A cavern system that can provide a route through the barrier. It is protected by Grandmother Moru, Beast of the Veiled Pass.

**Zpugglips**
A tribe of Bullywugs sarped by the acid water from the volcano.
