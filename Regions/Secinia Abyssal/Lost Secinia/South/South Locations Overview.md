Factions:
**Coils of Ydersius**
A Coils of Ydersius have founded a fort town of Chicahua, which is one of the only successful attempts at settling the island.

**B'Uhn'R'Uhn**
A tribe of grung that have a tentative alliance with Chicahua.

**Phubbip**
A tribe of bullywug that worship a tamed Froghemoth.



Locations:

**Ancient Statue**
A statue to an ancient deity long forgotten. A small tribe of monkey-goblins worship it.

**Astela**
An old settlement that was cursed. The townsfolk always rose again after death.

**Astela Graveyard**
To combat their curse, the people of Astela buried their dead on the island off the coast of Lost Secinia.

**Balturm**
The ruins of a Wine Haven established for the rare acidic flavour of the grapes of the jungle. Turns out the flavour was from the presence of a black dragon.

**Black Dragon Lair**
The lair of an adult black dragon. He lurks throughout the jungle in his valley, and lures adventurers into the River Serpent Temple. His lair is in a large crashed luxury airship.

**B'Uhn'R'Uhn**
The lake lair of the tribe of grung of the same name.

**Chicahua (Chick-a-wah)**
The fortress town of Ydersian settlers. They have a tentative alliance with the native grung tribe.

**Jade Eye Beacon**
An lighthouse built by the Ydersian settlers to guide ships to Chicahua.

**Naga Temple**
A temple with an immortal naga and their followers.

**Phubbip**
A settlement of grung that worship a tamed Froghemoth.

**River Serpent Temple**
A temple to a long forgotten river serpent spirit. The local black dragon uses it as bait for adventurers.

**Stonecleft Beacon**
An ancient lighthouse that still works through arcane means. It is inhabited by a clan of harpies led by Queen Xynevis. She has a fierce rivalry with the eyrie across the water.

**Tomb of Tlacotl**
The tomb of an ancient Yuan-Ti lich from the days of Secinia proper. Since the fall, she knew she would not have the soul to maintain her lichdom, and made the preparations to become a demilich. She is now a floating snake skull with gemstones for eyes.
