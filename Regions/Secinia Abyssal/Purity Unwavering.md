
Purity Unwavering is a Sea Elven Enclave in the northern reaches of Secinia Abyssal. It has closed down its borders to outsides, and is not allowing travel into or out of the city. This is due to the ongoing lycanthropy outbreak that has begun to infect numerous citizens. 



**Sea Elven Pride**
Due to the pride of the sea elves, they want the situation thoroughly contained and the problem solved before any information makes its way outside of the enclave. To this end, anybody infected with the wolf-eel lycanthropy has been sealed away in the prison and temple to Iomedae in the tunnels beneath the enclave.

**Important NPCs:**

**Superior Cyncus**
Cyncus is a 287 year old male sea elf. He has extremely long, straight pale blue hair, and pale green skin.
He is the Superior of Purity Unwavering, position in charge of the Enclave.
He was recently infected with Wolf-Eel Lycanthropy as part of a plot to assassinate him by triton insurgents.

**Sage Eos.**
Eos is a 346 year old female sea elf. She has long white hair and pale blue skin. She has a thin, wirey build that appears malnourished.
The de-facto leader of Purity Unwavering after Cyncus became infected with Wolf-eel Lycanthropy.
