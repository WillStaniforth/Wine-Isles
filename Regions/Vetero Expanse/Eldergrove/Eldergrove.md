Eldergrove is an independent city within the Vetero Expanse. It is one of the largest cities on Ordumenion, and sits on the coast at the southern end of the Silken Strait. 

It is defined by the enormous oak tree known as the groveheart that towers over the city, sheltering it beneath its boughs. The tree and the city have both stood for over a thousand years, though few claim to remember it's origins. The firbolgs are the keepers of the groveheart, and are a common sight around the city. They are treated with reverence and respect, with locals making way for them in the street and offering gifts at the shrines throughout the city.

The city has seven districts within its walls, including the groveheart itself.

[[Groveheart]]
The Groveheart is the tree at the centre of Eldergrove. It was lovingly grown over a millennia by generations of firbolgs, and protected by [[Orduvanthia the Tender]].

[[Starheart Canopy]]
Starheart canopy is the wealthiest district within the city. It is mostly built up of manors to house the nobles of the city, as well as some of the more up-market stores.

[[Moonroot Hollow]]
Moonroot hollow holds the merchants of Eldergrove, with a wide arrays of shops and taverns. Most days a sprawling market opens in the bustling highstreet.

[[Rootreach Commons]]
Rootreach commons houses the less wealthy members of the city. While they may not have enough money to build lavish houses, the district is well maintained by the firbolgs so that nobody is left without or run down.

[[Brighthollow]]
Similarly to Rootreach commons, Brighthollow houses a large amount of the citizens of Eldergrove. Being close to the West gate and the docks, it is often the place to find lodgings for visitors to the city.

[[Sunglint Rise]]
Home of the Sunglint gardens, this wealthy district is a pleasure to behold. 


[[Timberfell Verge]]
Timberfell Verge is the closest that Eldergrove has to a docks district. While there are no official docks, the workers make do with temporary jetties of floating branches.
