Starheart canopy is home to the six major noble houses of Eldergrove.


***House Adris.*** 
A haughty house of high elf wizards. They tend to keep to themselves as best they can. They are responsible for the day-to-day organisation and beurocracy of the city.


***House Flickerfern.*** 
This clan of wood elves have historically had a lot of power within the city, but due to a scandal a generation ago, they power has been weakened. No longer in control of the guards of the city, they instead control the docks and all other trade throughout the city (outside of material resources).


***House Matrosk.*** 
A human house that is proud of their station on the council among the longer-lived races. They are now in charge of the guards within the city.


***House Starforge.*** 
A clan of mountain dwarves that claim to have Azer heritage. They are exceedingly talented smiths, and are patrons to the local crafting guilds. They traditionally control imports and exports of resources from the city.


***House Wyrnralei.*** 
A proud family of bronze dragonborn. House Wyrnralei have existed in eldergrove for hundreds of years, and historically have been the ambassadors between the city and [[Orduvanthia the Tender]]. With the disappearance of Orduvanthia, the stability of the house has been thoroughly shaken and their power in the city undermined.


***House Zumec.*** 
A busy family of wood gnomes that live closest to the [[Groveheart]]. They are responsible for religious affairs within the city, as well as communing with the firbolg.
